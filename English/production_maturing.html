NPI Phase

The NPI phase brings a product from proof of concept, to a functioning prototype, further to mass production.

Each new product will go through 3 phases below:

Prototype maturing

  • Prototype build, P1
  • Prototype build, P2

Certification

Mass production batch, MP

  • Pilot production, PP

Contact us, we will be pleased to send you a project proposal.

Key data from clients

Key Procedure

BOM (Bill of Material) review

  • Reduce price and lead time with a BOM review
  • Consider component alternatives more easily available in China, without compromising on quality

DFM (Design for manufacturing) review of product design

  • Ensure manufacturability
  • Plan test and assembly procedures into product design considerations
  • Foresee and prevent problems before production

QA (quality assurance) plan

  • Agree with client and factory on programming, testing and QA process
  • Secure production and testing reliability

Traceability

Supplying a global market is challenging, as regulations vary from country to country and new regulations updates from time to time.

To hold each delivery's specifications under control, we help define and implement traceability system, so you can easily identify each delivery and trace the root of manufacturer.
