SMATE ENGINEERS

SMATE is an independent consultancy with our unique experience in manufacturing in China, we offer consultation to companies that embraces the potential benefits of manufacturing in China.

As a team of experts in our field, we help clients solve challenges accross various aspects, cost-effective compoenents, reliable manufacturer partner and extend their product line to many more.

We often gets into early-stage projects that has functioning prototypes, ready to production, that need a BOM review, a cost down, a DFM, a test evaluation, a good documentation for production, that need an affordable lab for certification, a good project manager that move productions under control and foresee/prevent problems from all aspect during production.

SMATE is set up in China in 2015 and set up in Denmark in 2019.

Cooperation

We have different versions of cooperation where the tasks get resolved by combining one or more of these:

  • Consultant Service: Our consultants are part of the customer's team in the customer's offices or manufacturer
  • Full projects: SMATE performs the task for the customer as a full project or in single stages
  • Total deliveries: SMATE manufactures and delivers the finished prototype, production equipment or finished products
