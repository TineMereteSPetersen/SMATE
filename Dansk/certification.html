SGS

Certification need to be planned early in the NPI process to avoid delays and trouble during compliance testing. Take advantage of our experience and close connection with SGS in Shenzhen who provides very cost-effective certification services for a wide range of fields and countries, such as CE, FCC, IC, CCC, battery-related safety certification, material testing, IP-rating and RF tests.

Doing certification in Shenzhen typically cost a fraction of European test labs due to high competition, lower labour costs, and very high throughput of products. The key is close cooperation, early risk assessment and pre-testing by renting labs per hour.

Orientation test

When you are not certain that you can pass the tests, we recommend you to run trail test for, e.g EMC, RF, if the orientation test pass, it will not be tested again for the complete product.

Pre-test

Pre-test examples: 200 point 3D antenna pattern measurement, absolute power and harmonics, IP-rating, material tests

E.g. metal surface treatment and contamination + layer thickness

SGS and we will help to advise on preparation and how to test + what is needed besides functioning prototypes.
