Overview

It can be challenging to be price competitive, however, if you are working with the right partner, the possibility of benefiting from cheaper supplies is significantly bigger.

Sourcing is one of our best strength, we navigate through the jungle of Chinese manufacturers, thrive at achieving objectives including target price, product specifications, certifications, speed-up delivery, as well as supplier research, qualifications and analysis. We have comprehensive knowledge of general possibilities and risks, and how to balance between the two, we are confident to be your OPTIMAL SOURCING PARTNER.

It's not mandatory to have sufficient specifications before a sourcing task, we will discover what's available in the market, what's a good solution and make recommendations.

It doesn't matter if you don't have any experience with China, we will guide you through all the process and you will master quickly and benefit always.

CHALLENGES CUSTOMER ENCOUNTER

Manufacturer or trader

Chinese factories that our clients find on Google are often very big manufacturer, however, sometimes could also be small trader with good marketing.

Emergency

Phone calls plays a dominant role in communications when you are in need of attention, we makes endless calls to get help from factory’s decision-makers

3M tape

We experienced a few times where clients suspected their 3M tape is not genuine and we proved they were right by digging into the case.

Cost

It’s understandable to ask for English speaking manufacturers, however, the price goes rocket up and it’s the worst disadvantage for large quantities.
